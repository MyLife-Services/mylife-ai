About MyLife Membership
Empower your digital self with MyLife, a nonprofit humanist member organization whose core mission is to provide you with the tools to digitally manage and preserve what truly matters to you, for now, throughout your lifetime, and for posterity.
Your personal Memory Team of intelligent agents will help you
  • collect your memories for yourself, loved ones and posterity
  • improve, sharpen and bolster your memories with images and recollection prompts
  • interactively relive your own memories
  • contribute your memories for the people and causes you care about
  • explore public memories from across cultures, language and time
  • build memories together as partners or communities
Your contributions are precious and your contributions will change the world for the betterment of all of us.
